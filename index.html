⚔️ Boss Night Team Builder

Import sign-ups, generate teams, drag to adjust, then copy and paste into Discord.

Step 1 – Import sign-ups

Paste one line per player. Format: @Name (Vocation) BossCodes. Example: @Blu Yei (EK) PZFHL or Alice (MS) PZ (ED) FHL.

Players and sign-ups

Add roles (e.g. Red Knight, Mentor) and set vocation priority per boss if a player has multiple vocations for the same boss.

Step 2 – Generate teams

Enter boss codes and how many teams per boss. Examples: P2 Z1 F1 (2 Pale, 1 Zelos, 1 Feru), P1 Z1 H1 L1 C1.

Step 3 – Preview and adjust

Drag players between teams or reorder. When you’re happy, copy the formatted text below and paste it into Discord.
