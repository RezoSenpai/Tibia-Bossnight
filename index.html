⚔️ Boss Night Team Builder

Import sign-ups, set priority, generate teams, drag to adjust, then copy into Discord.

1. Import

One line per player: @Name (Vocation) BossCodes

Imported characters

Set priority (0 = first). If you sign up with multiple vocations for the same boss, use «boss» prefer: to choose which one the script picks first.

ℹ️ How teams are built

Selection order: The script picks players in this order: first by priority (0 = highest, picked first), then by vocation preference if you signed up with multiple vocations for the same boss, then by name. Anyone who doesn’t fit in a team becomes a back-up.

Complete team: A team that meets the minimum vocation and size rules below. If there aren’t enough sign-ups, a partial team is still shown (with ⚠️); you can use it or adjust manually.

Underfilled teams: If you ask for 2 teams but one ends up with very few players (e.g. only 1), the script merges everyone into one team, puts the lowest-priority overflow in back-up, and hides the empty team.

Requirements per boss (complete teams)

“Shooters” = MS + RP combined.

  • Pale (P) — Max 10. Min 1 EK, 1 ED, 2 shooters. No special roles.
  • Zelos (Z) — Max 10. Min 2 EK, 2 ED, 2 MS, 2 RP. Max 3 EK. If 3 EK: min 1 RP + 3 ED. Red Knight / Main tank = 1 EK+ED combo (assign 2 in the small box; they get [Red Knight] in copy).
  • Ferumbras (F) — Max 15. Min 2 EK, 2 ED, 1 MS, 3 shooters. (If more EK, ~70–80% should have Sio; 1 EK puts Feru in spot — not enforced by script.)
  • Heart of Destruction (H) — Max 15. Min 3 EK, 3 ED, 1 MS, 5 shooters. Max 6 EK. Prefer 1 ED per EK. Mini teams: 1 EK/ED combo + 3 others (5 per group). Left, Middle, Right.
  • Last Lore Keeper (L) — Max 15. Min 4 EK, 4 ED, 1 MS, 5 shooters. Mini teams: 1 EK/ED combo each. Top Left, Top Right, Bottom Left, Bottom Right.
  • Cults (C) — Max 10. Min 1 EK, 1 ED, 1 RP, 2 shooters. EK/ED combo for boss, RP for Yalahari.
  • The First Dragon (D) — Max 15. Min 2 EK, 2 ED.

Preferred setup

Teams are filled to boss size respecting min/max. HoD = 3 boxes (Left, Middle, Right); LLK = 4 boxes. Virgins: prefer same native language in mini team (manual). Copy = one message per team with sections inside.

2. Generate teams

Boss codes + count: P1 Z1 F1 = 1 Pale, 1 Zelos, 1 Feru.

3. Preview & copy

Drag players between teams. Empty teams are ignored when you copy. Always one extra team slot so you can add more.
